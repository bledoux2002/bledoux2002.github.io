About Me

 I am a senior undergradute student at Connecticut College majoring in Computer Science. As a member of the Ammerman Center for Arts and Technology, I have shaped my studies around the intersection between art and tech. Specifically, I am studying the design and development of video games and other interactive media.

 My current studies include researching different diegetic methods of guiding users in virtual environments, developing an open-source tool for the Unity engine that procderually generates tilemaps, and creating environments in virtual reality.

 As for personal projects, I am currently developing a terminal-based character creation and management program for the tabletop roleplaying game Fallout 2d20 by Modiphius Entertainment, a Quake-style FPS in a post-apocalyptic setting, and an independent port of the card game "Caravan" from Fallout: New Vegas. Both projects are being developed on the Unity Engine.
